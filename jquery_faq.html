Here are some Frequently Asked Questions about National Parks...
1. Who is the Director of the National Park Service?
Dan (the Man) Smith, of course.
2. What are concessions?
Concessions -
a. a thing that is granted, especially in response to demands; a thing conceded.
b. a preferential rate or allowance given by an organization.
3. Can I bring a pet to a National Park?
You can to some. Not to some others though.
4. What do I need to know about driving off-road in a National Park?
Lots of things. Before you head out, check with the national parks that you intend to visit. In many national parks, off-road driving is illegal, bruh.
5. How do I find out about contracting opportunities?
Visit www.grants.gov.
6. How many units are in National Parks?
419 absolute units.
7. Does every state have a National Park?
Technically, no.
8. What is the world's largest National Park?
Greenland.
9. What is the smallest National Park?
Hot Springs, AR
10. Which National Park is visited the least?
Gates of the Arctic National Park and Preserve, AK
